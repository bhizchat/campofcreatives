Backend / Infra & SRE (GameLift • Containers • CI/CD • Observability)

Location: San Francisco, California Team: Engineering Job Type: Full-time

What to Expect

You’ll design the autoscaling backbone that spins up fleets of dedicated servers for synchronized shows, with safety, observability, and cost controls built in.

What You'll Do

  • Stand up Amazon GameLift (or equivalent) with target- and rule-based autoscaling; codify capacity policies.
  • Build containerized game server images and blue/green rollouts; instrument metrics, tracing, and alerting.
  • Own feature flags, configuration, and incident response; drive SLOs for showtime readiness.

What You'll Bring

  • Experience running high-availability, latency-sensitive backends and GameLift fleets.
  • Proficiency in IaC, CI/CD, and service mesh or ingress patterns.
  • Implement data flows and controls aligned to GDPR principles (lawful basis, minimization, rights requests).
  • Build COPPA-aware experiences, including verifiable parental consent, data retention, and limits on targeted ads to under-13s.
  • Create privacy reviews, DPIAs, and audit-ready logging across services.
  • Hands-on with privacy engineering and child-safety requirements for consumer realtime apps.

Encouragement

Even if you don’t check every box or have all the experience listed, we encourage you to apply if you feel you’d be up to the job and you’re willing to learn.
