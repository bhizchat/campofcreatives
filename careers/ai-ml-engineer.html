AI/ML Engineer – Virtual Try-On & Avatar Personalization

Location: San Francisco, California Team: Engineering (AI) Job Type: Full-time

What to Expect

You’ll take state-of-the-art virtual try-on research and turn it into delightful, performant features—think photo booths and fast avatar wardrobe swaps during live events.

What You'll Do

  • Prototype and productionize diffusion-based virtual try-on models (e.g., TryOnDiffusion), with distillation for speed and latency control.
  • Build inference services, safety filters, and A/B pipelines around personalization.
  • Collaborate with Character Tech to map outputs to runtime-ready materials and meshes.

What You'll Bring

  • Publications or shipped features in generative vision (try-on, inpainting, diffusion) and strong MLOps discipline.

Encouragement

Even if you don’t check every box or have all the experience listed, we encourage you to apply if you feel you’d be up to the job and you’re willing to learn.
