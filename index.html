About

On April 25th 2020 Fortnite and Travis Scott hosted one of the biggest events in Gaming History.

It was called Astronomical, a concert on a Fortnite map that had over 12.5 million players attend and viewed over 250 million times.

At the time Epic Games were the only company able to throw such a virtual event at this scale.

But not anymore, see at Camp of Creatives we've been obsessed with this idea of virtual spaces for a while. We've always had a different view of the world. We had a collective dream.

We wanted to know what it would take to throw the biggest concert in the world. Like a party for everyone. And now we believe with generative AI we can build a digital world that could contain over 100 million people by 2032.
