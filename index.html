About

Afro Japan is an original game concept that fuses ancient samurai codes with modern Hip‑Hop culture. The core vision is to immerse players in a stylized world where neon‑lit cities and traditional villages exist side by side—and the ethos of warrior clans intersects with the competitive energy of rap battles.

The experience is structured around three modes:

  • Story Mode — Players begin as a reckless orphan from a struggling rural village who travels to the city for work and food. Survival means navigating rival clans of modern samurai, each with its own code, style and deadly rivalries. Through missions, the protagonist earns respect, joins the Dream Clan, and finds a family.
  • Online Mode — Starts with virtual concerts and grows into an open‑world multiplayer space where players join clans, collaborate on missions and experience live music events.
  • Vs Mode — Competitive head‑to‑head battles. Friends face off using characters inspired by real‑world rappers, channeling the hype of rap battles into stylized samurai duels.

Afro Japan frames Hip‑Hop’s competitive spirit through samurai lore—clans behave like rap crews, alliances and rivalries drive stakes, and themes of mentorship, loyalty and respect transform an outsider into a respected clan member.
